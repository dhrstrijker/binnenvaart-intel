05 — Motion

Animation & Interaction

Micro-interactions that feel effortless. Every transition is smooth and intentional, using spring-based easing for an organic quality.

Skeleton Loading
Soft shimmer effect moves across placeholder shapes while content loads. The warm sand-to-linen gradient feels natural.
Card Hover Lift
Hover me! Cards lift with a gentle translateY(-8px) and shadow bloom using cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1).
Spring Filter Pills
All Motor Tanker Duwbak
Click the pills! Filter toggles use spring easing cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) for a bouncy, tactile feel.
Page Transitions

Fade + subtle 16px upward slide on route changes. Duration: 400ms with ease-out curve. Content staggering: each card appears 50ms after the previous one for a cascading waterfall entrance.

Price Morphing

When a price changes, digits roll with a 300ms staggered animation. The old price fades to 50% opacity with a strikethrough, while the new price enters from below. A subtle pulse on the trend badge draws attention.

07 — Rationale

Why Nordic Clean

A design direction built for trust, clarity, and the confidence that comes from knowing exactly what you are looking at.

Trust Through Restraint

Vessel purchases are high-stakes decisions. The generous whitespace and muted palette create a calm environment where buyers can think clearly, compare specs, and make confident choices without visual pressure.

Data Clarity

With 447 vessels and complex specs, information hierarchy is everything. The type system and component design ensure that vessel names, prices, and key dimensions are instantly scannable, even on crowded listing pages.

Maritime Without Cliche

Instead of dark navy and rope textures, we channel the feeling of water through subtle color choices and fluid motion. The sage and ocean tones evoke inland waterways without resorting to nautical kitsch.

Premium Positioning

Navisio aggregates from four brokers. The refined aesthetic positions it as the authoritative marketplace: the place professionals go first. The design says "we take this industry seriously" without saying a word.
